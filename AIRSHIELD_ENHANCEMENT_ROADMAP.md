### 1. AI-Powered Smart Recommendations

#### 1.1 Personalized Activity Suggestions
- **Feature**: AI-driven recommendations for optimal outdoor activities
- **Implementation**: 
  - Weather and AQI integration for activity timing
  - Historical user behavior analysis for personalized suggestions
  - Integration with fitness APIs (Apple Health, Google Fit)

  - "Perfect weekend for hiking - conditions excellent"

- **Feature**: Real-time route optimization for air quality
- **Implementation**:
  - Google Maps/Waze integration with AQI overlay
  - Predictive routing based on historical pollution patterns
  - Integration with public transportation schedules
- **Benefits**: Reduce exposure during daily commutes

#### 1.3 Dynamic Health Score
- **Feature**: Real-time health score calculation based on multiple factors
- **Components**:
  - Current AQI exposure
  - Personal health conditions (asthma, allergies)
  - Activity level and intensity
  - Duration of exposure
- **Output**: Personalized risk assessment and recommendations

#### 2.1 Multi-Sensor Ecosystem
- **Feature**: Support for various air quality sensor types
- **Supported Devices**:
  - Professional monitoring stations (Purple Air, AirVisual)
  - IoT sensors (ESP32-based custom devices)
  - Industrial-grade sensors for workplace monitoring
  - Vehicle-mounted sensors for mobile monitoring

#### 2.2 Sensor Calibration System
- **Feature**: AI-powered sensor accuracy verification
- **Components**:
  - Cross-reference with government monitoring stations
  - Automatic calibration adjustments
  - Sensor health monitoring and alerts
  - Fleet management for deployed sensors

#### 2.3 Wearable Integration
- **Feature**: Smartwatch and fitness tracker integration
- **Capabilities**:
  - Continuous AQI monitoring on wrist
  - Vibration alerts for poor air quality
  - Heart rate correlation with air quality exposure
  - Workout adjustments based on air quality

### 3. Community Intelligence Network

#### 3.1 Crowdsourced Data Validation
- **Feature**: Community-powered air quality verification
- **Mechanisms**:
  - Photo verification of readings
  - User-reported pollution sources

  - Gamification for community contributions

#### 3.2 Neighborhood Air Quality Reports
- **Feature**: Hyperlocal community monitoring
- **Components**:
  - Street-level air quality mapping
